Recent projects

'ExperinceART' project aim was to explore how interactive technology could be used to create innovative experiences that would be memorable and meaningful to the visitor. I used Arduino, Processing, projections, sensors, physical objects and scenography to turn the space into an immersive Artist's studio.

We Share is an Irish web-based sharing service where people can swap, lend, borrow and give-away items and share skills and knowledge. It's a great concept that needs a lot of improvement in user experience and usability of the service, so there came the idea to re-design this service as a mobile application. In this project, we used Illustrator, Photoshop and Figma.
